## Positioning note on "luxury"

Your inventory spans premium villas *and* modest-market apartments (Azua, Baní, San Gregorio de Nigua). Leading every headline with "luxury" over-promises. Recommended framing:

> "From city apartments to luxury villas — Dominican Republic real estate for every goal."

Use "luxury" as a **tier/filter**, not the blanket description of the whole marketplace.

## "Verified" — define it

Wherever "verified" appears, link it to a short explainer so it's a substantiated claim, e.g.:

> **How we verify:** Every listing is checked for clear title and confirmed by the listing agent before publication. [Learn more]

Without this, "verified" is an unsubstantiated claim that could be challenged.
