# Meridian — Brand Voice & Style Guide (v1 draft)

A working brand voice document for Meridian, the bilingual (EN/ES) Dominican Republic real estate marketplace. Use it to keep copy consistent and to run `/brand-review` against real standards instead of a generic check.

---

## 1. Brand personality

If Meridian were a person, they'd be a **well-connected local expert who happens to speak both languages fluently** — someone who knows the difference between Cap Cana and Bávaro, walks you through the title process without jargon, and tells you plainly when a deal isn't right for you. Knowledgeable, trustworthy, and calm. Never a hype-driven salesperson.

---

## 2. Voice attributes

**Trustworthy**
- **We are:** transparent about process, costs, and risk; we substantiate claims and cite sources.
- **We are not:** vague, hype-driven, or promising guaranteed returns.
- **Sounds like:** "Closing costs in the DR typically run 3–5%. Here's the breakdown."
- **Does NOT sound like:** "Guaranteed 14% returns — invest now before it's gone!"

**Bilingual & local**
- **We are:** equally fluent and natural in English and Spanish; grounded in real DR geography and law.
- **We are not:** a translated-once afterthought or a generic Caribbean brand.
- **Sounds like:** "Verified across the DR — from Santo Domingo to Samaná."
- **Does NOT sound like:** literal, awkward machine translation.

**Clear & practical**
- **We are:** plain-language, step-by-step, jargon-defined.
- **We are not:** dumbed-down or fluffy.
- **Sounds like:** "As a foreigner, you can own property outright. Here's how."
- **Does NOT sound like:** "Navigate the multifaceted paradigm of cross-border asset acquisition."

**Confident, not boastful**
- **We are:** assured and specific.
- **We are not:** superlative-stuffed ("best," "only," "#1") without proof.
- **Sounds like:** "One of the most active listing platforms focused on the DR."
- **Does NOT sound like:** "The #1 real estate site in the Caribbean."

---

## 3. Audience

- **Primary:** foreign buyers and investors (US, Canada, Europe) purchasing DR property for lifestyle, rental income, or diversification.
- **Secondary:** Dominican buyers/renters; sellers, agents, and brokerages who list on Meridian.
- **They care about:** legal safety as a foreigner, real costs and taxes, honest yield expectations, and trustworthy listings. Address them as capable adults making a serious financial decision.

---

## 4. Messaging pillars

1. **Verified & trustworthy** — every listing is title-checked and agent-confirmed.
2. **Truly bilingual** — built natively for EN and ES, not translated.
3. **Made for the DR** — deep local coverage, law, and market knowledge.
4. **For buyers *and* investors** — lifestyle and returns, with honest framing.

Every page should reinforce at least one pillar without contradicting the others.

---

## 5. Tone by context

| Context | Dial up |
|--------|---------|
| Listings / search | Clear, factual, benefit-specific |
| Blog / market news | Authoritative, sourced, educational |
| Investment content | Honest, risk-aware, disclaimered |
| Seller/agent plans | Confident, ROI-specific, professional |
| Errors / support | Empathetic, blame-free, helpful |

Voice attributes stay fixed; only the emphasis shifts.

---

## 6. Style rules

- **Numbers:** spell out one–nine, numerals for 10+; always numerals for prices, yields, %, and measurements.
- **Percent:** use `%` (e.g., "6%").
- **Currency:** state the currency explicitly (USD vs. DOP) — DR prices are often dollar-denominated; never leave it ambiguous.
- **Oxford comma:** yes.
- **Headings:** sentence case.
- **Contractions:** yes (warm, human).
- **Exclamation marks:** avoid; let facts carry weight.
- **Dates:** `Month DD, YYYY` in EN; `DD de mes de YYYY` in ES.
- **Place names:** use correct local spelling and accents (Samaná, Baní, Santiago de los Caballeros).

---

## 7. Terminology

| Use this | Not this |
|----------|----------|
| sign up (verb) / sign-up (noun) | signup (verb) |
| verified listing (define it) | verified (undefined) |
| rental yield (sourced, dated) | guaranteed returns |
| foreign buyer | alien / foreigner (in body copy, prefer "foreign buyer") |
| Dominican Republic / the DR | "DR" on first mention without context |

**Inclusive & careful language:** avoid "easy" (say "straightforward"); avoid unqualified superlatives; frame investment upside with risk.

---

## 8. Claims & compliance rules (always enforce)

- **No guaranteed returns.** Yields and ROI are ranges, must be **sourced and dated**, and must sit near the investment disclaimer.
- **Substantiate "verified," "expert," and any superlative** or soften the wording.
- **Attribute economic stats** (GDP, foreign investment, interest rates) to a named source with a date.
- **Site-wide disclaimer:** "Meridian is a property marketplace and does not provide financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. Property values, rental yields, and returns vary and are not guaranteed. Consult a licensed professional before purchasing or investing."
- **Match promotion to inventory** — never promote markets/tiers you can't currently deliver without a "coming soon" label.
