**Day 18 — Facebook/Instagram (Educational — taxes)**
What are the taxes on DR real estate? 🇩🇴

When you BUY:
→ Transfer tax: 3% of property value
→ Legal/notary fees: ~1–2%
→ No additional stamp duty

When you OWN:
→ Annual property tax (IPI): 1% of value above ~$155K
→ Exemption: tourism zones (Law 158-01)

When you SELL:
→ Capital gains: 27% on profits (waived for primary residence)

Much simpler than most people expect.

Full guide at investwithmeridian.com/blog
