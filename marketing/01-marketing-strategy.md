# Meridian — Marketing Strategy
**investwithmeridian.com · June 2026**

---

## Situation

Meridian is a luxury real estate platform targeting the Dominican Republic market. The product is live, bilingual (EN/ES), and includes property search, listings, messaging, saved searches, and subscription tiers for agents/brokers. You have two distinct audiences: **foreign investors** (primarily US diaspora and international buyers) and **lifestyle/vacation buyers** (affluent individuals seeking DR homes). Budget: $100–$500/month.

---

## Core Positioning

**Tagline:** *Where real wealth meets the Caribbean.*

**Investor angle:** The DR is the fastest-growing economy in Latin America, with rental yields of 6–10% in Punta Cana and Cap Cana — outperforming most US markets after fees. Meridian gives investors private access to off-market deals.

**Lifestyle angle:** Luxury villas, penthouses, and beachfront condos in one of the world's top vacation destinations — now accessible from a single curated platform.

---

## Audience Profiles

### Audience A — Foreign Investor
- US-based Dominican diaspora (2.2M in the US) aged 35–60
- Non-Dominican foreign nationals interested in LatAm diversification
- HH income $150K+; already owns at least one property
- Searches: "invest in Dominican Republic real estate", "DR rental yield", "Cap Cana investment"
- Channels: LinkedIn, Facebook Groups, YouTube (financial channels), email newsletters

### Audience B — Luxury Lifestyle Buyer
- US/Canadian/European 45–65 seeking vacation home or early retirement
- May also be Dominican nationals living abroad
- Searches: "luxury villas Punta Cana", "beachfront homes Dominican Republic", "buy property DR foreigner"
- Channels: Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube, Google Search, travel/lifestyle blogs

## 90-Day Go-to-Market Roadmap

### Month 1 — Foundation (Free + Minimal Spend)

**Week 1–2: Own your SEO foundation**
- Submit sitemap.xml to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools
- Claim and complete Google Business Profile for "Meridian Real Estate"
- Publish Blog Post 1: "How to Buy Property in the Dominican Republic as a Foreigner (2026 Guide)"
- Set up free Ubersuggest or Google Search Console tracking

**Week 3–4: Social presence launch**
- Create Instagram (@meridian.dr or @investwithmeridian) and LinkedIn Company page
- Post first 12 social posts from the content calendar (included in this package)
- Join 5 Facebook Groups: DR expat groups, DR investment groups, Dominican diaspora groups
- Share the investor one-pager in those groups (see deliverable)

**Spend this month: $0–$50** (optional: $50 Meta ad to boost a single high-quality property post)

---

### Month 2 — Amplify

**Content engine:**
- Publish Blog Post 2: "Dominican Republic Real Estate Investment: Yields, Taxes & ROI (2026)"
- Publish Blog Post 3: "Punta Cana vs Cap Cana vs Las Terrenas: Which Is Right for You?"
- Continue posting from social calendar (30 posts)
- Begin email list — add signup CTA to homepage, use welcome sequence (included)

**Outreach:**
- DM 10 Dominican real estate agents/brokers on Instagram — invite them to list free
- Pitch to 2 YouTube channels that cover DR expat life or LatAm investing (offer free platform access)
- Submit Meridian to these free directories:
  - Trovit (trovit.com.do)
  - MitulaCasa
  - Expat.com property listings
  - internations.org local groups

**Paid spend: $100–$200/mo**
- Meta (Facebook/Instagram): $100 campaign targeting US + Canada + Spain users interested in "Dominican Republic", "real estate investing", "Caribbean lifestyle". Objective: website traffic or lead form. Run the investor one-pager as the creative.
- Google: optional $50 for exact-match keyword "buy property Dominican Republic foreigner"

---

### Month 3 — Scale What Works

**Analyze:** Which blog post got the most traffic? Which social post got the most engagement? Double down.

**Partnerships:**
- Reach out to 3 DR law firms (notaries/lawyers) — offer them a free Lawyer account on Meridian in exchange for mentioning Meridian to clients
- Partner with 1–2 DR tourism/relocation blogs for a guest post swap
- Reach out to RE/MAX Dominican Republic and Century 21 brokers to list on Meridian

**Paid: $200–$400/mo**
- Scale the winning Meta ad creative
- Test a Google Display campaign targeting lookalike audiences
- Consider $99/mo Zillow PR listing (for US visibility)

## Competitive Moat

Most DR real estate is fragmented across WhatsApp groups and local broker websites. Meridian's advantage is:
- **Bilingual platform** — captures both EN and ES audiences
- **Tech-first** — search, filter, messaging, saved requirements, all in one place
- **Subscription model** — aligns incentives with serious agents/brokers
- **Off-market positioning** — premium feel drives premium clients

Lean into this in all messaging. You're not another listing site. You're the *definitive* platform.
