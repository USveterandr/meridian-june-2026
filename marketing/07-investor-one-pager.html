Government Investment Incentives

  • Law 158-01: 10-year tax exemption on rental income, property tax, and capital gains for tourism-zone properties
  • Law 171-07: Permanent residency granted for investments of $200K+ in DR real estate
  • Foreign Ownership: Same rights as Dominican citizens — freehold title, no restrictions
  • Capital Gains Tax: Primary residence exempt; 27% on investment property gains
  • Annual Property Tax: 1% on value above ~$155K; exempted in tourism zones
  • No Wealth Tax: No estate or inheritance tax for foreign nationals
